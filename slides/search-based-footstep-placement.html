Search-based footstep placement

  • Search over action graph of either
    • Footsteps
    • Body positions
  • Discrete set of reachable footsteps
  • No timing or dynamics
  • e.g. Chestnutt et al. [5, 6], Veranza et al. [7], Winkler et al. [8], Zucker et. al. [8]
